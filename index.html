About Our Server

Hello, and welcome to the Collegiate SMP, a (somewhat) fun local minecraft survival server from our school we use to hang out, host events, and have fun! We consist of good minecraft pvpers, awesome builders, and a well balanced player-base.

Server Features

  • ["Money/Economy Driven Server"]
  • ["Community Events and Competitions!"]
  • ["(Almost) 24/7 Uptime, and constant backups up to 3 times a day!"]
  • ["Competetive Survival Server, and Builder Oriented!"]
  • ["Java, Bedrock, and Eaglercraft Compatible Server!"]
  • ["Tiering System - The better you are, the more valuable you get!"]

Getting Started

The main target of our survival server are mainly players from around our real-life local area, but players all around the world are welcome to join, too! Just go to the Quick Links and look at client links, or join our discord!
